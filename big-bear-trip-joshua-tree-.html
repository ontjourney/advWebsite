Embark on an incredible 48-hour journey that takes you from the mystical desert landscapes of Joshua Tree National Park to the pristine mountain peaks of Big Bear Lake. Whether you're seeking stunning sunsets over ancient Joshua Trees or fresh mountain air at 7,000 feet, this itinerary has it all.
